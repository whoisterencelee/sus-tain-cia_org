Introduction to getting your NFT, you will need to follow these steps, try to click below each section, let us know if you have difficulty.

On your desktop/laptop computer, go to metamask.io and install MetaMask Browser Addon / Extension

You should see this after it has been installed, click "Get Started" and follow the instruction to "Create a Wallet"

During this setup process, you will be asked to create a password, in addition there will be a secret recovery phrase generated

Please remember to **write down your secret recovery phrase**, this will be your keys and the only way to recover your wallet

At the end of the setup, you should see a "Congratulations", you have a digital wallet now, let's try to use it

Now go to opensea.io , at the top right corner, click the profile symbol or wallet symbol or the hamburger menu , if you selected the hamburger menu click on "Connect wallet" and select metamask

You will be asked to Connect With MetaMask, click Next and Connect, this will let opensea know about your wallet

Now you should see your wallet address/ID which is a string on the top right that is formatted like "0x????...????", click on that to copy the address, you can always find your wallet address from the wallet symbol or the hamburger menu and entering My Wallet

You need to paste this wallet receiving address when you reply to the email, so that we can send the NFT to your wallet address We will send you the NFT In the meantime, check out our twitter for more artworks and give us a follow

Using the same computer / browser which you installed wallet on previously

Check you can see your NFT, it might be hidden, follow these steps or Go to opensea.io/account?tab=private , you might be required to signin with the metamask wallet

Next, click on the three dots and then "unhide"

Click on the Heart ❤️ as shown here, you might be required to sign message with the metamask wallet

Now you have enabled the full power of NFT , show your friends the NFT with share button
Did you remember to follow twitter.com/sustaincia this is where the NFT lives socially

Advance:
To see the NFT directly within the Metamask wallet on your phone you need to:
- install Metamask on your phone
